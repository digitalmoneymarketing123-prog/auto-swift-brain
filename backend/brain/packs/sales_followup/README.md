\# Sales Follow‑Up Brain Pack



The Sales Follow‑Up Brain Pack improves the quality, clarity,

and response rate of post‑meeting sales communication.



This pack applies structured decision logic to prevent weak,

vague, or ineffective follow‑ups.



\---



\## When to Use This Pack



Use this Brain Pack after:

\- Discovery calls

\- Sales meetings

\- Product demos

\- Proposal discussions



It is designed for professionals who want consistent,

high‑quality follow‑ups without sounding scripted.



\---



\## What This Pack Does



\- Enforces a single objective per message

\- Ensures clear calls‑to‑action

\- Maintains professional confidence

\- Prevents common follow‑up mistakes

\- Improves likelihood of response



\---



\## What This Pack Avoids



\- Generic or vague follow‑ups

\- Apologetic language

\- Over‑explaining

\- Multiple CTAs

\- Aggressive escalation



\---



\## How It Fits Into Auto‑Swift Brain



This Brain Pack sits above the core framework and LLM adapters.



It can be combined with any supported LLM without changes

to the underlying system.
