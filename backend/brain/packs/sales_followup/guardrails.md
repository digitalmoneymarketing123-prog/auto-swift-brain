\# Sales Follow‑Up Brain — Guardrails



Guardrails enforce quality, professionalism, and consistency.

They prevent common mistakes that reduce response rates and damage credibility.



\---



\## Guardrail 1: No Multiple Calls‑to‑Action



A follow‑up message must contain \*\*only one CTA\*\*.



The following are NOT allowed:

\- Asking for two time options AND feedback

\- Asking for confirmation AND additional documents

\- Combining scheduling with pricing discussion



One message = one decision.
